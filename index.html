Joonhyuk Yang Hello and welcome!

I am an assistant professor of marketing at the Mendoza College of Business, University of Notre Dame. My research interests are at the intersection of marketing and technology. I currently focus on topics related to the digital economy, such as platforms, advertising, and AI/ML applications to managerial and policy questions.
